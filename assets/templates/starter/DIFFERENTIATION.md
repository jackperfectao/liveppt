# Anti-Clone Style Guardrails

This starter intentionally avoids direct replication of creator-provided visual systems.

## Must Keep (LivePPT Signature)

- Structured panel layout (rail + stage + metric zone).
- Low-radius geometry and enterprise readability.
- Token-based theming with content-structure separation.

## Must Avoid

- Dominant light-glass card language as the main style motif.
- Oversized rounded bubble layers as core composition.
- One-to-one reuse of animation choreography, timing, or scene sequence.
- Reusing custom icon composition patterns as brand identity.

## Design Distance Checklist

- Layout skeleton differs from source style by at least two regions.
- Motion family differs in both direction and pacing.
- Card shape, borders, and depth system are independently defined.
- Theme palettes are tokenized and not color-matched to source branding.
